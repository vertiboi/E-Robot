01

Open To All Students

Senior high school and college students are eligible to join the competition. Participants must be able to provide proof of their enrollment status upon request.

02

Register

Participants are expected to register before the designated deadline and must provide all necessary information.

03

Objective

The project to be created is a line-following robot that will race on a line track. The objective for all participants is to build the fastest line-following robot.

04

Restrictions

The competition has no restrictions on the tools, applications, or software that participants may use.

05

The Kit

The robotics kit and line-track will be provided by Erovoutika.

06

Elimination

The competition will be based on the winners per race and will be running a single elimination format. The robots will be paired and will race against each other, losers will be eliminated, winners will advance in the tournament.

07

Sports
manship

By participating, all participants automatically agree that they vow to respect fellow participants and staff.
